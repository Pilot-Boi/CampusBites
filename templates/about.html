About CampusBites

CampusBites is your campus community platform for discovering and sharing food events. Connect with fellow students, find free food on campus, and never miss out on campus dining opportunities.

College is hard enough - between classes, assignments, extracurriculars, and trying to maintain a social life, keeping track of where and when free food is available shouldn't add to the stress. As fellow students, our goal is to make campus life a little easier and a lot more delicious.

We understand that student budgets are tight and campus dining can be expensive. That's why we created CampusBites - to help you discover club meetings with pizza, department events with catering, and all those hidden opportunities for free food across campus. More than just saving money, it's about building community and making sure no student goes hungry.

Join our community today and never miss another campus food event. Because good food brings people together, and together we can make campus life better for everyone.
